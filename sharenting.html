Sharenting

Sharenting is a term used to describe parents oversharing about their children online. The term can tend to have negative connotations when it is generally just relating to parents wanting to share information about their children.

As the first generation of kids brought up into this life are now creating their own online profiles, it is beginning to be questioned if they are creating their own online presence or developing off of the one their parents already created for them.
