Further Reading

If you would like to look further into how to share online safely, some links have been provided.

Childline - Staying Safe Online

This website provides information about how to remain safe online. It provides simple bullet pointed solutions broken down into sections.

Norton - 5 Tips for Safe Sharing

These tips from Norton detail how to recognise when information could be too much information. Through knowing this it describes when sharing becomes oversharing.

Childnet - Safe Sharing

This website defines what personal information is, how it could be shared through different avenues, and top tips. This page is aimed at children.

Stay Safe Online

Tips broken down into bullet points. Describes relevant information about how information is used, how to take action, how to keep their information safe.
